Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

Shrimp fried rice is a quick and tasty meal made with cooked rice, shrimp, scrambled eggs, and mixed vegetables. It's a great way to use leftover rice and comes together in just a few minutes using one pan.

The dish is customizableâ€”swap in your favorite vegetables or adjust the seasoning to taste. It's a convenient and satisfying option for lunch or dinner, especially when you're short on time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups cooked rice (cold or day-old is best)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, scramble the eggs until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add more oil if needed. Stir-fry peas, carrots, and green onions for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add rice and soy sauce. Stir well and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Return shrimp and eggs to the pan. Mix everything together and cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm.
