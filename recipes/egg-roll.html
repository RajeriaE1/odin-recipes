Shrimp Egg Rolls

Description

This shrimp egg roll recipe combines chopped shrimp with shredded cabbage, carrots, and green onions for a simple, flavorful filling. The mixture is cooked with soy sauce and garlic, then wrapped in egg roll wrappers and baked or fried until golden brown.

These egg rolls can be served as a snack, appetizer, or light meal. They go well with dipping sauces like sweet chili or soy sauce. The recipe is easy to customize by adding other vegetables or seasonings based on preference.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled, deveined, and chopped
  • 2 cups shredded green cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • 8-10 egg roll wrappers
  • Cooking oil (for frying or brushing if baking)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the filling: Sauté garlic, cabbage, carrots, and green onions in a pan. Add chopped shrimp, soy sauce, and optional sesame oil. Cook until shrimp is pink and mixture is well combined. Let cool slightly.
  2. Assemble the rolls: Place 2-3 tablespoons of filling in the center of each egg roll wrapper. Fold and roll tightly, sealing the edges with a bit of water.
  3. Cook the egg rolls:
    • To fry: Heat oil in a pan and fry egg rolls until golden brown on all sides.
    • To bake: Preheat oven to 400°F, brush rolls with oil, and bake for 15-20 minutes, turning once.
  4. Serve: Let cool slightly and serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
